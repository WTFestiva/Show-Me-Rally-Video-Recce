2024 Show-Me Rally Video Recce

This event will be held under the current American Rally Association (ARA) Rules. The rules and bulletins can be found at: ARA Rules and Bulletins. The ShowMe Rally (SMR) will consist of one day of competition and is part of the ARA Central Regional Series. This regional event will also count toward the Eastern Ozark Rally Cup, which is a two event series of Missouri rallies (100AW, SMR).

  • A route book will also be provided to each competitor, course notes will not be provided.
  • The route will be approximately 225 miles in length with approximately 70 miles of competitive stages.
  • All competitive stages will be held on roads that consist primarily of gravel, and will vary somewhat in width and tightness.
  • Roads vary from sweeping, well-groomed roads to tight and twisty.
